Deep Woods Security, LLC

Secure your business one bit at a time!

Deep Woods Security, LLC is a cybersecurity firm specializing in penetration testing services for small to mid-sized businesses. Our mission is to deliver comprehensive penetration testing solutions to safeguard your company's and customers' sensitive data. Based in Coastal Virginia, we are committed to helping organizations strengthen their security posture and protect against evolving cyber threats.

Services Provided

Web/API Application Testing

This service focuses on finding vulnerabilities in websites or APIs (application interfaces) that could be exploited by hackers. We test the security of your apps by simulating attacks to ensure sensitive data and functionality are safe from breaches..

Network/Host Testing

Here, we evaluate the security of your IT infrastructure, including servers, computers, and network devices. We look for weaknesses that could let unauthorized users access your systems and work to prevent them.

Red Teaming Engagements

This is a full-scale simulation of a real-world attack where we act like hackers trying to break into your organization. Our goal is to test your overall security response and highlight gaps that need improvement.

Phishing/Social Engineering Campaigns

These are controlled attacks where we test how well your employees can spot and handle phishing emails and other forms of trickery used by attackers to gain sensitive information. This helps strengthen human defenses against cyber threats.
